Product Return Policy

At our online store, we want to ensure your satisfaction with every purchase. If you are not completely satisfied with your order, we offer a hassle-free return policy to make things right.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, the following conditions must be met:

  • The item must be unused and in the same condition as you received it.
  • It must be in the original packaging.
  • You must have the original receipt or proof of purchase.
  • The return must be requested within 30 days of the delivery date.

Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support team to notify them about the return.
  2. Package the item securely and include all original accessories.
  3. Include a copy of the receipt or proof of purchase.
  4. Ship the item back to our return address.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you about the status of your refund. If the return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original payment method. Please note that it may take several business days for the refund to be processed and appear in your account.
